Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any of our courses directly through our website. Visit the Courses page, select the course you're interested in, and click the "Enroll Now" button. You'll be guided through the registration and payment process. If you need any assistance, feel free to contact us via this form or by phone.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) as well as PayPal. For corporate or group enrollments, we also accept bank transfers - please contact us for details.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with a course?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all our courses. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our support team within 14 days of enrollment for a full refund, no questions asked.

Do you offer in-person classes or consultations?

Currently, all our courses are offered online. However, we do offer limited in-person consultations and workshops at our Fremantle location. Please contact us to inquire about availability and scheduling.

How can I become an instructor at Stayvia?

We're always looking for talented fashion professionals to join our instructor team. Please send your CV, portfolio, and a brief teaching proposal to careers@stayvia.org. Our team will review your application and get back to you if there's a potential match.
