Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2020, Stayvia has quickly become Australia's premier online destination for fashion and style education. What began as a passion project between three fashion industry veterans has grown into a comprehensive learning platform with thousands of students across the country.

Our team brings together decades of combined experience in fashion styling, trend forecasting, retail buying, and sustainable fashion. We've worked with major Australian brands, styled celebrities, and consulted for international fashion houses - and now we're bringing that expertise directly to you.

At Stayvia, we believe that great style should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background or budget. Our courses are designed to demystify fashion and empower you to develop your unique style with confidence.

Our Mission

To democratize fashion education by providing affordable, accessible courses that combine industry expertise with practical skills. We're committed to helping Australians develop their personal style and pursue careers in fashion.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity in fashion for all body types and backgrounds
  • Sustainability through responsible consumption
  • Innovation in teaching methods and content
  • Authenticity in all our recommendations
  • Community building among fashion enthusiasts
