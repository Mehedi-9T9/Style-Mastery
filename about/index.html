Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2020, Stayvia has quickly become Australia's premier online destination for fashion and style education. What began as a passion project between three fashion industry veterans has grown into a comprehensive learning platform with thousands of students across the country.

Our team brings together decades of combined experience in fashion styling, trend forecasting, retail buying, and sustainable fashion. We've worked with major Australian brands, styled celebrities, and consulted for international fashion houses - and now we're bringing that expertise directly to you.

At Stayvia, we believe that great style should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background or budget. Our courses are designed to demystify fashion and empower you to develop your unique style with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Stayvia and what do you offer?

Stayvia is an online platform offering premium fashion and style education courses based in Australia. We provide structured courses taught by industry experts covering various aspects of fashion, styling, wardrobe building, and sustainable fashion practices.

Our courses are designed for both beginners looking to improve their personal style and professionals seeking to enhance their fashion knowledge.

Who are your courses for?

Our courses are designed for:

  • Individuals looking to improve their personal style
  • Aspiring fashion stylists
  • Fashion students wanting supplementary education
  • Professionals in related fields (retail, marketing, design)
  • Anyone interested in sustainable fashion practices

We offer courses at different levels from beginner to advanced.

How are your courses different from free content available online?

While there's plenty of free fashion content online, Stayvia courses offer:

  • Structured, comprehensive curriculum developed by experts
  • Professional instruction with personalized feedback (in premium tiers)
  • Practical assignments to apply what you learn
  • Downloadable resources and worksheets
  • Certificate of completion for your portfolio
  • Access to our community of fashion enthusiasts

Enrollment & Access

How do I enroll in a course?

Enrolling in a Stayvia course is simple:

  1. Browse our available courses on the Courses page
  2. Select the course you're interested in
  3. Choose your preferred pricing plan
  4. Complete the enrollment form with your details
  5. You'll receive immediate access to the course materials

If you have any trouble enrolling, please contact our support team.

How do I access my courses after purchasing?

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with login credentials to our learning platform. All your enrolled courses will be available in your dashboard immediately.

If you don't receive the email within 15 minutes:

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Ensure you entered the correct email address
  • Contact our support team at support@stayvia.org
Can I access courses on mobile devices?

Yes! Our learning platform is fully responsive and works on:

  • Smartphones (iOS and Android)
  • Tablets
  • Laptops and desktop computers

You can access your courses anytime, anywhere with an internet connection. Some courses also offer downloadable materials for offline viewing.

Pricing & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfers (for annual plans)
  • Afterpay (for Australian residents)

All transactions are processed securely through our payment partners.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Important notes about cancellation:

  • We don't charge cancellation fees
  • No refunds for partial months
  • You can resubscribe anytime
  • All your progress and course materials will be saved
Do you offer discounts or scholarships?

We occasionally offer seasonal discounts and promotions. To stay updated:

  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Follow us on social media
  • Check our website regularly

For students and those experiencing financial hardship, we offer a limited number of partial scholarships each quarter. Please email scholarships@stayvia.org with your request and circumstances.

Course Content

Are the courses suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our courses are designed to accommodate all skill levels. Each course clearly indicates its difficulty level, and many include beginner-friendly modules that provide foundational knowledge before progressing to more advanced concepts.

We recommend starting with:

  • Fashion Styling Fundamentals
  • Wardrobe Essentials
  • Color Theory for Fashion

These courses provide excellent foundations before moving to more specialized topics.

How much time should I dedicate to each course?

Course duration varies, but here are general guidelines:

  • Short courses (4 weeks): 2-3 hours per week
  • Standard courses (6 weeks): 3-5 hours per week
  • Comprehensive courses (8+ weeks): 5-7 hours per week

All courses are self-paced, so you can complete them faster or slower as needed. The time estimates include watching lessons, completing assignments, and reviewing materials.

Do you offer certificates upon completion?

Yes, all our courses include a certificate of completion that you can download after:

  1. Completing all video lessons
  2. Submitting all required assignments
  3. Passing the final assessment (if applicable)

Certificates include:

  • Your name
  • Course title
  • Completion date
  • Instructor name
  • Unique verification code

These can be shared on LinkedIn or included in your professional portfolio.

Support & Refunds

What if I'm not satisfied with a course?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our courses. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase:

  1. Contact our support team within 30 days of enrollment
  2. Let us know why the course didn't meet your expectations
  3. We'll process a full refund, no questions asked

For subscription plans, you can cancel anytime as described in our Terms of Service.

How do I get help if I'm having technical issues?

Our support team is here to help with any technical issues:

  • Email: support@stayvia.org (response within 24 hours)
  • Help Center: Comprehensive articles and troubleshooting guides
  • Live Chat: Available weekdays 9AM-5PM AWST

Before contacting support, please:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Try refreshing the page
  3. Clear your browser cache
  4. Try a different browser
Do you offer instructor support?

The level of instructor support depends on your subscription plan:

  • Basic: Access to community forums where instructors occasionally participate
  • Standard: Priority email support with responses within 48 hours
  • Premium: Direct messaging with instructors, personalized feedback on assignments, and bi-weekly Q&A sessions

All students can ask questions during live sessions (when available) or in the community forums.
